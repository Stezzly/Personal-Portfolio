About me

Hello, I'm Stephen!

I love everything tech—whether it's hardware, software. My passion for technology drives me to constantly explore new tools and techniques to create immersive digital experiences.

From Curious Beginnings...

My journey into the world of tech started at a young age. I was always fascinated by how things work—why software behaves the way it does and what makes hardware tick. This curiosity soon turned into a full-blown passion for building things myself.

Currently Studying & Growing

Right now, I’m studying at Memorial University and Keyin College, where I'm learning the ins and outs of computers. These experiences have not only honed my technical skills but have also fueled my creativity in solving complex problems.

Building My Dreams: Rockland Gaming

Outside the classroom, I spend my time as a game developer and entrepreneur, working on my own company, Rockland Gaming.

The goal?

To create fun, innovative, and impactful gaming experiences that resonate with players. I’m actively working on developing games and pushing my limits to build a company that I can be proud of.

What Drives Me?

I'm driven by a passion for creating something from nothing. Whether it’s coding a new game feature, troubleshooting an issue, or testing a new idea, I thrive when I see my work come to life. I believe technology has the power to change the world, and I want to be a part of that change.
